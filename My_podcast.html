The Back on Track Podcast

All aboard The Back on Track Podcast, the ultimate station stop for train enthusiasts, history buffs, and anyone who’s loco for locomotives! Hosted by Train Guy, a teenage rail fanatic with a passion that’s off the rails, this podcast will take you on a first-class journey through the fascinating world of Irish trains.

From the chugga-chugga beginnings of steam engines to the sleek, modern trains zipping through Ireland today, Train Guy leaves no stone (or ballast) unturned. He’s got the grit to tackle the big questions: Why did the train get so good at math? (It knew all the angles!) And what do you call a train loaded with bubblegum? (A chew-chew train!)

Every episode is packed with whistle-worthy stories about historic routes, iconic locomotives, and the people who’ve kept Ireland’s railways chugging along. Plus, Train Guy sprinkles in updates on today’s rail network and future plans – because he’s full steam ahead when it comes to sharing what makes the tracks tick.

So, whether you're a veteran trainspotter, a curious traveler, or just along for the ride, The Back on Track Podcast is here to keep you on track with laughs, learning, and a whole lot of heart. Don’t miss the train – hop aboard today!
